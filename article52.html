THERE'S RIO! Teen Mom fans catch rare glimpse of Kailyn Lowry’s son Rio’s face in blink-and-you’ll-miss-it moment at gender reveal

"I feel like it was ripped out from under me time and time again."

By Jackie Whetzel

TEEN Mom fans have caught a rare glimpse of Kailyn Lowry's son Rio.

The blink or you'll miss it moment was captured during a special family moment at the Teen Mom 2 star's gender reveal party for her twin babies.

BABY NUMBER SIX AND SEVEN

The Teen Mom star announced that she was expecting her sixth and seventh babies during an episode of her podcast, Barely Famous, in late October.

Less than a week later, two sources exclusively confirmed to The U.S. Sun that Kaily had officially given birth to the twinsies.

The former MTV star shares both Rio and the twins with her boyfriend Elijah Scott, 25.

She also has four other sons: Isaac, 13, with her ex, Jo Rivera; a son, Lincoln, nine, with her ex-husband, Javi Marroquin; and sons, Lux, six, and Creed, three, with ex Chris Lopez.

On Wednesday, the reality star shared a second video from the twin's gender reveal party on Instagram.

A large pink and blue balloon arch with a bright white sign that read: "Twins," was displayed near a gleaming pool.

A GIRL FOR KAIL?

"I'm hoping that they're both girls," Kailyn's oldest son Isaac said, while looking into the camera at the beginning of the video.

"I think it's going to be boys," her second oldest son, Lincoln then said, counteracting his older brother's statement.

After several of Kail's young sons took turns giving their predictions on what they thought their siblings would be, the camera panned to the Teen Mom 2 star standing with five of her children.

She was holding Rio in the photo.

Cannons then shot out blue smoke to reveal the sex of the babies.

A source previously told The U.S. Sun exclusively that Kail was expecting a boy and a girl, however, the video insinuated that the reality star was having two more boys.

'THEY'LL JUST BE MORE BOYS'

"I think my mom should stop having kids," her son Isaac teased during the video, adding that: "They'll just be more boys ... and more and more!"

Kailyn quietly welcomed Rio in November 2022 - almost a year prior to the twin's birth.

She has shared very little about her fifth son, which was her first child with boyfriend Elijah.

Last month, Kail talked about her decision to keep Rio's birth secret, telling People: "I wanted to be able to tell my own story on my own terms and kind of share what information I wanted to share instead of being within a contractual obligation or storyline."

RIO'S PRIVATE LIFE

She said that she wasn't ready to announce Rio's birth when she did, however, speculation from the public forced her to spill the news.

"I feel like it was ripped out from under me time and time again," she told the outlet.

"I kind of feel like my hands are tied. People are announcing it for me and so, at this point, I might as well just talk about it."

"At this point, I can at least try to do it my way," she added.

Despite being forced to share information about her son, the former MTV star said Rio was a pleasant surprise.

She also confirmed that she and Elijah were not trying to get pregnant when he was conceived.

"As chaotic as it was finding out about being pregnant again, he's been such an incredible baby that it really was a true light at the end of a really dark year for me," she told the same outlet.

"It was rough [last year] with the lawsuit, the depression that I dealt with and everything else. So, he has been really a joy for all of us."
