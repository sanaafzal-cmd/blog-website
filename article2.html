SHE'S DON ‘Nikki has no chance’ rages Donald Trump at town hall as he slams Haley for not dropping out & hints at his running mate

By Jackie Whetzel

Trump also challenged Joe Biden to 'as many debates as necessary' to win the 2024 election

DONALD Trump has lashed out at Nikki Haley for not dropping out of the 2024 presidential race despite failing to win a single primary.

The former president also gave voters a hint at who his potential running mate could be at a town hall in South Carolina on Tuesday.

It will be the second voyage of the Good Vibrations cruise, but the first with award-winning actor and musician John Stamos on board. Stamos is most famous for his starring role as “Uncle Jessie” on Full House.

“The second voyage of the Beach Boys Good Vibrations Cruise will feature live music from sunup to sundown, across several stages and settings throughout the ship, including two unique shows from the Beach Boys and performances by a spectacular selection of legendary guests including a special sail away set from The Isley Brothers and open sea concerts from The Temptations, The Righteous Brothers, Mark McGrath, RAIN – A tribute to the Beatles, Jimmy Webb, Katrina (from Katrina and the Waves), Maggie Rose, Kris Allen, The Surfajettes, Morgan James, and the Neon Queen – Tribute to Abba,” the release read.

In addition to the epic lineup of entertainment, the Good Vibrations cruise will offer guests a chance to interact with some of these famous musicians and will also offer every cabin on board a chance to take a photo with the Beach Boys, the release noted.

The cruise will also feature two special theme nights, including a Mardi Gras theme, where guests are encouraged to break out their best Bourbon Street style, and a Dancing Queen night, where bell bottoms and disco vibes will be celebrated.

The Beach Boys have partnered with Sixthman, the creators of festivals on sea and sand, for this event. For more information, visit thebeachboyscruise.com.
